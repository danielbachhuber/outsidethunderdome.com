DFM wins 27 Local Media Editorial Awards

Digital First Media newspapers were recognized with 27 awards in this year’s Local Media Associated Editorial Contest. Categories ranged from Best Investigative Reporting to Best Column Writing to Best Front Page.

“Our news organizations can only be successful if they cover their local communities better than anyone, so taking home so many awards in the Local Media Association is not only something we’re proud of, but something that validates our efforts to effectively serve our communities,” said Editor-in-Chief Jim Brady.

Here are the awards won by Digital First Media newspapers:
