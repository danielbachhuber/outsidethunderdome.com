Davis Shaver (Technology Strategist)

Davis Shaver is a Technology Producer for Digital First Media's Project Thunderdome.

He has been with the company since October 2012. Prior to that, Davis had been an undergraduate at the Pennsylvania State University, where he created the student news website OnwardState.com. Davis served as the site's chief leader through his four years at Penn State, which granted him honors in History and Science, Technology, and Society upon graduation.

Davis began his career in journalism as a high school student covering municipal meetings for the Lebanon Daily News, his hometown paper. He has worked previously for AOL, PBS MediaShift, Lazerpro Digital Media Group, and Penn State in roles related to technology and operations. He currently lives in New York City.
