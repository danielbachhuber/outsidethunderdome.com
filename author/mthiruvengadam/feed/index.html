I started my journalism career as an intern at a Digital First Media property – The Salt Lake Tribune – and that’s part of why I wanted to join Thunderdome. My experience in Salt Lake City prepared me to seize opportunities and cover some amazing stories around the world.

In the years since my internship, I’ve written about everything from murders and mayhem on the streets of San Antonio to the Federal Reserve, the European financial crisis and the Northern Lights in Iceland. I’ve developed a love for the pace and scope of news wires, an appreciation of the local communities newspapers serve and a desire to help make the news product more meaningful to its consumers. Thunderdome gives me a unique opportunity to serve all of those interests at once.

As business channel manager, I get to keep my eye on big national stories while serving local audiences and developing an innovative news product. It’s like I get to have my cake and eat it too. And the fact that I’ll be working to help the paper that gave me my start develop even stronger business coverage is the icing on that cake.
