Mark Lewis (Operations/Communications Director)

Mark Lewis is the Communications/Operations Editor for Project Thunderdome, a centralized team that produces high-quality non-local journalism for Journal Register Company and Media News Group properties on all platforms.
 
Prior to his current role, Mr. Lewis served as Editorial Products Manager for Journal Register Company where he worked across the editorial and product organizations. He joined Journal Register Company in 2009 as corporate multimedia editor.
 
Mr. Lewis began his career as a reporter, photographer and editor at newspapers in Tennessee, Arizona and Missouri. He holds an MA in convergence journalism from the Missouri School of Journalism.
 
Mr. Lewis lives in Langhorne, Pennsylvania.
